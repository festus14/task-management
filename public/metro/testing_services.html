1. National Pension Commission (PENCOM) Audit
      1. Employer’s Pension Registration
      2. Assist with monthly remittance of Pension contributions
      3. Process and obtain annual Compliance Certificate 
      4. Pre-audit review/audit exercise
      5. Post audit reconciliation/resolving query issues (if any)
      6. Advisory support services on employees’ Pension contributions
      7. Give an up-to-date advise on changes in the Pension Reform Act
  2. Industrial Training Fund (ITF) Audit
      1. ITF Registration 
      2. Offer timely advise on ITF contributions
      3. Assist with remittance of ITF contributions
      4. Process and obtain annual Compliance Certificate
      5. File the Company’s annual ITF returns
      6. Pre-audit review/audit exercise
      7. Post audit reconciliation/resolving query issues
      8. Give an up-to-date advise on changes in the Industrial Training Fund Act
  3. Nigeria Social Insurance Trust Fund (NSITF) Audit
      1. NSITF Registration
      2. Monthly remittance of NSITF contributions
      3. Process and obtain annual Compliance Certificate
      4. Filing of the Company’s NSITF returns
      5. Pre-audit review/audit exercise
      6. Post audit reconciliation/resolving query issues
      7. Give an up-to-date advise on changes in the Employees’ Compensation Act
  4. National Housing Fund (NHF) Audit
      1. NHF Registration
      2. Monthly remittance of NHF contributions
      3. Filing of the Company’s NHF returns
      4. Pre-audit review/audit exercise
      5. Post audit reconciliation/resolving query issues
      6. Give an up-to-date advise on changes in the National Housing Fund Act
  5. Miscellaneous Taxes/Levies
      1. Update the Company on changes in any Labour and/or Employees related Tax laws and regulations
